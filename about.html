Est.1978

Kingdom began it's journey from local roots. Each place has streams in form all over the country. Kingdom started with silk weaving in hometown Haiyan, the cornerstone on which local people from Yangtze River delta all depend.

In the 1980's companies relied on direct experience and a determined spirit. Kingdom was founded using handmade wooden looms that are the icon of that age. It took Weiming Ren four years to rise from a technician to become the factory directoru. He created the first all-in-one production line covering all aspects of the process. Like a flower blooming out of the dust, Kingdom has stepped on a long way of exploration.





”All of this is due to my love and devotion to the job, I cherished it so much.”

Mr. Ren Weiming (任維明)
Chairman and Executive Director of KINGDOM.

Location

Kingdom Ethiopia Linen PLC is strategically situated within the dynamic landscape of Adama Industrial Park in the Oromia Region, Ethiopia. This prime location places Kingdom Ethiopia at the heart of industrial innovation and economic growth. Adama Industrial Park, with its modern infrastructure and forward-looking environment, provides an ideal setting for Kingdom Ethiopia's operations. The park's strategic position in Adama not only ensures access to key resources and amenities but also underscores Kingdom Ethiopia's commitment to contributing to the industrial vibrancy of the region and the nation as a whole
